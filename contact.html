Common questions before you write

🐛 Found a bug?

The fastest way to get a bug fixed is to open an issue on GitHub. Include your device, browser, and steps to reproduce the problem.

🎓 Want to use HirenJS in class?

You don't need permission — it's MIT licensed. But we'd love to hear about it! Tell us how you're using it for students.

💡 Have a feature idea?

Open a GitHub discussion or fill in the contact form with your idea. Community suggestions have shaped many features of HirenJS.

🔒 Privacy concern?

We take privacy seriously. Read our Privacy Policy or contact us directly and we'll respond promptly.
